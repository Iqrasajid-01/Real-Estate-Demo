Our Story

Building Dreams, One Home at a Time

Founded in 1999, Premium Estates has grown from a small family-owned brokerage to one of the most respected real estate companies in the nation. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional service and help families find their perfect homes.


Today, we continue to uphold those values while embracing innovation and technology to serve our clients better. Our team of over 200 experienced agents works tirelessly to ensure every transaction is smooth and successful.
